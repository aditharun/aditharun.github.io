Small Things To Work On

Sometimes I find myself needing a break from things, and have found working on small tangible projects to be very relaxing. The queue of small projects:

  • An R pipeline that takes raw idat files for a given tissue and turns them into a vector representing chromatin states in that tissue. Presumably data comes from a public source like TCGA.
  • Analytics for group chats. Some things of interest to gather: who talks the most or the least? when is the most activity (weekends, nighttime, etc.)? Who responds to who's texts most often? Average response time to a text?
